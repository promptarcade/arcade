**FEEDBACK:**
1. **Shape is the #1 problem.** The silhouette must read as a human torso, not a jar. Wider at shoulders, narrower at waist, belt at natural waistline. Reference the profile function from the arm exercise — use cubic interpolation control points for shoulder → chest → waist → hip.
2. **V-neck collar needs to be a visible feature.** At 64px, the V-neck should be 8-10px tall with clear skin showing through. Currently it's a tiny dark notch.
3. **No fabric texture is present.** The progress notes document that multi-scale hash noise + sine drape modulation work for fabric. Apply them — the smooth gradient is a regression from the arm exercise.
4. **Missing shoulder seams.** Two visible seam lines from collar to shoulder edge would define the garment's construction and break the barrel silhouette.
5. **Edge pairs absent.** Every material boundary (collar, belt top, belt bottom, sleeve attachment) needs dark crevice + bright ridge. Currently they're single-tone lines.
6. **No material differentiation.** Fabric, leather, and skin should render differently — different specular behavior, different texture frequency. Currently they're all smooth gradients in different colors.
7. **Warm/cool hue shift missing.** The blue palette should shift toward warm (teal/cyan) in highlights and cool (purple/indigo) in shadows. The belt should shift from warm ochre to cool brown.
