# Epoch Tap — Tap Through Time

## Concept

An evolutionary idle/clicker that spans from the first amino acids to interstellar colonisation. Each tap generates **Spark** — the universal currency of progress. The defining mechanic: early eras crawl (millions of years compressed into minutes of play), but as civilisation advances, the pace compounds exponentially — mirroring humanity's real acceleration curve. The Stone Age lasted 3 million years. The Space Age took 60. Your playthrough will feel that.

No fail state. No going backwards. Just relentless forward motion.

## Art Style

**Chibi** (32-56px) — warm pixel art that evolves with the eras. The palette shifts organically: volcanic reds → ocean blues → earthy browns → stone grey → bronze gold → medieval jewel tones → industrial soot → electric neon → cosmic violet. Each era should feel like a different world.

## Prestige System: Extinction

After reaching any era past Era 4, or after completing the game, you can trigger an **Extinction Event**. This resets all progress back to Era 1, but awards **Legacy Points** based on how far you got.

**Legacy Points** provide permanent bonuses across all runs:
- +5% all production per point
- Every 10 points: unlock a permanent upgrade (see below)
- Points earned: Era 4 = 1, Era 5 = 2, Era 6 = 4, ... Era 12 = 256, Victory = 500

**Permanent unlocks:**
- 10 LP: Auto-Tap (1 tap/sec automatic)
- 25 LP: Speed Start (begin Era 1 with 100 Spark)
- 50 LP: Bulk Buy (x10 and x100 buy buttons)
- 100 LP: Offline Boost (offline progress x2)
- 200 LP: Era Skip (can skip Era 1 after first extinction)
- 500 LP: Cosmic Awareness (all generators visible from Era 1, greyed out)
- 1000 LP: Quantum Spark (taps generate Spark equal to 1 second of production)

**Extinction flavour by era:**
- Era 4: "An ice age descends..."
- Era 5: "The bronze collapses..."
- Era 6: "The empire falls..."
- Era 7: "Plague sweeps the land..."
- Era 8-9: "War consumes everything..."
- Era 10-11: "The climate shifts..."
- Era 12: "You choose to begin again, knowing what you know."

## Technical

- Single HTML file (no sprite engine — canvas art + DOM UI)
- Canvas: tap target area, background, particles, floating numbers
- DOM: all UI panels, buttons, numbers (faster updates than canvas text)
- requestAnimationFrame: canvas animations
- setInterval(100ms): production ticks (10x/sec for smooth counters)
- All numbers stored as JavaScript numbers until they exceed safe integer, then switch to string representation with custom math
- Generator cost formula: baseCost × 1.15^owned
- Upgrade costs: fixed per upgrade, shown in generator panel
- Breakthrough cost: fixed per era
